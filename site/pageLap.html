Lap Steel Information:


Welcome to the Bluestem Lap Steel Guitar pages.

Here you will find lots of information related to electric lap steel guitar construction and related accessories; please select from the links above for further information.



Bluestem lap steel features:

  • Figured maple body overlay
  • Ebony headstock overlay with mother of pearl "Bluestem" script logo
  • 22-1/2" standard, 24" and other custom scales
  • Gotoh enclosed deluxe tuners
  • Solid brass nut
  • Ebony fretboard with mother of pearl position markers
  • Standard string spacing of 1-3/4" or 1-7/8" at nut and 2" at bridge
  • 24 large profile 18% nickel silver frets
  • Solid brass bridge with integral stainless steel bridge plate, through body string mounting
  • Standard Bluestem split single coil pickup (high output and rich tone, but without the hum)
  • 500K quality audio taper volume control with integral treble bleed circuit and large custom volume knob
  • 1/4" Switchcraft output jack side mounted in custom polished stainless steel recessed jack plate
  • Stainless steel screws used at all body locations
