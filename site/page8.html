Lap Steel Information:


More examples of Bluestem Strings lap steel guitars

Some of the instruments produced in the past are shown here, and they serve as a way to illustrate the various combinations of headstock and body design, as well as the different pickups that I have used. Note that all of these instruments have been sold, and are only used for purpose of example. Please contact me if you are interested in obtaining an instrument, and be sure to check the "Lap Steels Currently Available" page.

Flamed maple natural finish lap steel #3329 below is SOLD:

Top view:

This lap steel features traditional body shape, modern headstock design, and is equipped with a P-90 pickup.

Blue maple 6 string with Rail humbucker #4434 below is SOLD.

Front view:

This lap steel features modern body shape and modern headstock design. It also incorporates a split rail humbucker for noiseless operation that can be switched to single coil mode for more edge.

Natural maple 8 String lap steel #6339 below is SOLD.

Top view:


This lap steel features traditional body shape, way more strings than I usually play, walnut fretboard with smaller gauge fret wire, walnut headstock overlay, and a Bluestem single coil pickup. I can't play C6 worth a darn, so the new owner will have to do it justice!
